About Me

Hello! I am Ali Hammed, a Physics graduate from Lagos State University with a passion for blockchain, Web3, and web development. I enjoy building smart contracts and exploring how web technologies can interact with blockchain systems.

I have completed courses in Solidity development through Cyfrin Updraft and successfully built two decentralized applications: a Crowdfunding DApp, which allows users to create campaigns, fund them with ETH, and withdraw funds once goals are reached; and a Token Saving Vault, which enables users to deposit, check balance, and withdraw ETH securely.

Alongside blockchain, I have also started learning web development because I strongly believe that mastering front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript will allow me to build more interactive and user-friendly DApps. My ultimate goal is to combine my skills in blockchain, web development, and problem-solving to create innovative solutions that can make a real-world impact.

I am always looking for opportunities to grow, collaborate with others, and contribute to exciting projects in the Web3 and tech space.
